# Movies & Entertainment

**Entertainment** is any activity that holds people's attention and provides enjoyment, amusement, or relaxation. It is a central part of modern human life. **Film (movies/cinema)** is one of its most powerful and universal forms.

---

## What are Movies?

A **film** is a sequence of photographs played rapidly enough to create the illusion of motion, typically combining images, sound, and music to tell a story or convey an experience.

Films are one of the most powerful forms of human storytelling — they can generate empathy, change minds, preserve culture, and create shared experiences across language barriers.

---

## The Film Industry

The global film industry is enormous:

- **Hollywood** (Los Angeles, USA) — the dominant global film industry; produces globally distributed blockbusters
- **Bollywood** (Mumbai, India) — world's most prolific film industry by volume; characterized by music, dance, emotion
- **Chinese cinema** — rapidly growing global presence
- **Korean cinema** — gained international recognition with films like *Parasite* (2019, first non-English film to win Best Picture at the Oscars)
- **European cinema** — known for art films, auteur directors (Federico Fellini, Ingmar Bergman, Jean-Luc Godard)

## Major Film Genres

| Genre | What It Is | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Drama** | Serious, character-driven | The Godfather, Forrest Gump |
| **Comedy** | Designed to make you laugh | Ferris Bueller's Day Off, The Grand Budapest Hotel |
| **Action/Adventure** | Physical excitement, battles, chases | Die Hard, Mad Max |
| **Science Fiction** | Speculative futures/technology | Star Wars, Interstellar, The Matrix |
| **Horror** | Designed to frighten | The Shining, Get Out |
| **Documentary** | Non-fiction; real events/people | Planet Earth, Super Size Me |
| **Animation** | Animated characters | Studio Ghibli films, Toy Story |
| **Romance** | Love stories | Casablanca, Titanic |
| **Thriller/Mystery** | Suspense; solving a puzzle | Hitchcock films, Knives Out |

## Beyond Film: Other Entertainment

### Television and Streaming
**TV shows** (series) have become as sophisticated as film. **Streaming platforms** (Netflix, Disney+, HBO Max, Amazon Prime, Apple TV+) have transformed how people consume video content — entire seasons available at once, watched at the viewer's pace.

Must-know cultural touchstones: *Breaking Bad*, *Game of Thrones*, *The Sopranos*, *Friends* (comedy), *The Office* (comedy)

### Video Games
An enormous entertainment industry; larger than film and music combined by revenue. Games range from simple mobile apps to complex narrative experiences. Major platforms: PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, PC.

### Books
Physical and digital printed narratives ([[Reading & Writing]]). Literature is one of humanity's oldest art forms and remains deeply relevant.

### Podcasts
Audio programs on any topic; listened to during commutes, exercise, etc. Enormously varied — news, storytelling, comedy, education.
