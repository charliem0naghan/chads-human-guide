# What is Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun in a solar system located in a galaxy called the **Milky Way**. It is the only planet known to support life — which is why it's so crowded.

---

## Basic Stats

| Fact | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age | ~4.5 billion years old |
| Diameter | ~12,742 km (about 7,918 miles) |
| Surface | 71% water, 29% land |
| Atmosphere | Mostly nitrogen (78%) and oxygen (21%) |
| Day length | 24 hours (one rotation) |
| Year length | 365.25 days (one orbit of the Sun) |
| Moons | 1 (simply called "the Moon") |

---

## The Surface

Earth's land is divided into 7 large masses called **continents**:

- **Africa** — the oldest inhabited continent, enormous diversity
- **Antarctica** — frozen, almost no permanent human population
- **Asia** — the largest continent, home to ~60% of all humans
- **Australia/Oceania** — includes Australia, New Zealand, and Pacific islands
- **Europe** — small but historically very influential
- **North America** — includes the US, Canada, Mexico, and more
- **South America** — includes Brazil, Argentina, Colombia, and more

These continents are surrounded by five **oceans**: the Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, Southern, and Arctic.

---

## The Atmosphere

The thin layer of gas surrounding Earth is called the **atmosphere**. It does several vital things:

- Provides **oxygen** for breathing
- Blocks harmful radiation from the Sun
- Traps warmth (the **greenhouse effect**) — life depends on this, though humans have been accidentally overdoing it lately ([[Climate & Environment]])
- Creates **weather** — rain, wind, snow, storms

---

## Day and Night

Earth spins on its axis. The side facing the Sun experiences **day** (light, warm). The side facing away experiences **night** (dark, cooler). This 24-hour cycle governs when most humans sleep, work, and eat.

---

## Seasons

Earth orbits the Sun on a tilted axis. This tilt causes **seasons**:

- When your half of Earth tilts *toward* the Sun → **Summer** (warmer, longer days)
- When your half tilts *away* → **Winter** (colder, shorter days)
- In between → **Spring** and **Fall (Autumn)**

Seasons are opposite in the Northern and Southern Hemispheres.

---

## Life on Earth

Earth is teeming with life — an estimated **8.7 million species** of plants, animals, fungi, and microorganisms. Humans are just one of these. They have, however, had an outsized impact on nearly all the others.
